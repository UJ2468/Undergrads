Elevating Tantashala From Local Handloom Store to a Digital First Fashion Brand

Branding & E-commerce for Sambalpuri Sarees

Overview

Tantashala is more than just a fashion label, it is a celebration of India's timeless handloom heritage. Based in Bargarh and Sundargarh, Odisha, the brand specializes in handwoven Sambalpuri sarees, kurtas, dresses, and artisan bags, all crafted by skilled local weavers. Although Tantashala's products embodied rich culture and craftsmanship, the brand lacked visibility and modern digital presence. That's where UnderGrads Media came in to bridge the gap between tradition and technology.
